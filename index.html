Locally Owned, Locally Loved

Medica Pharmacy is proud to be locally owned and deeply rooted in the Erie Street and Italian community. We are blessed to be part of an amazing community filled with wonderful people, and we will always aim to further support and develop the community through our care and actions.
We look forward to welcoming you to the Medica family!

What Makes Us Different

  • Fast Service: New prescriptions are often ready within minutes.
  • Personalized Care: Our pharmacist takes the time to listen, answer your questions, and ensure you fully understand your medications.
  • We’ll Come to You: If you have a prescription but can’t make it to the pharmacy, we’ll come to you. Our team will pick up your script and deliver your medications once they’re ready.
  • Always Here for You: We’re open seven days a week, including holidays, so there’s always someone here to help when you need it.
