Pharmacist Consultation

Our pharmacists are always happy to speak with patients to answer questions and address any concerns. We offer medication reviews to help you better understand your prescriptions and ensure they’re working effectively for you. To accommodate your needs, our pharmacists are available for consultations over the phone or in person, in the private consultation rooms at Medica Pharmacy.

Minor Ailments Care

Our pharmacists are qualified to assess and prescribe for a variety of everyday health concerns, making care more accessible when you need it.
We can help with:

  • Common ailments: seasonal allergies, pink eye, heartburn, pregnancy-related nausea, and mouth-related issues such as cold sores or thrush.
  • Skin-related concerns: acne, rashes, eczema, dermatitis (including diaper rash), insect bites, and hives.
  • Women’s health: uncomplicated urinary tract infections, yeast infections, period pain, and hemorrhoids.
  • Pediatric issues: pinworms and skin irritation in young children.
  • Minor injuries and preventative care: muscle sprains, joint strains, and tick bites.

Don't hesitate to give us a call if you have any specific questions.

Blood Pressure Readings

All patients are welcome to stop by the pharmacy to use our blood pressure monitoring equipment at their leisure. In addition, our pharmacists will help understand these blood pressure readings and make lifestyle recommendations to achieve patient goals.

Anticoagulation Management

We offer anticoagulation services for patients taking blood thinners such as warfarin.
This includes:

  • Regularly scheduled INR monitoring
  • Immediate dose adjustments (no need to wait for a doctor's appointment)
  • Personalized education and lifestyle recommendations to achieve target goals

Speak with a pharmacist for more details.

Weekly & Bi-Weekly Blister Packs

Blister packs, individually handmade and verified by our staff, organize your medications into clear, labeled slots for each time of day (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Bedtime), one or two weeks at a time. These are recommended for any patients with complex or confusing medication regimens in order to assist with correct administration and reduce the risk of errors.
Interested in switching to blister packs? Talk to one of our pharmacists today.

Free Home Delivery

We offer free medication delivery for patients who are unable to visit the pharmacy in person.
Standard delivery hours are:

Monday-Friday 10am-6pm
Saturday 10am-3pm
Sunday 11am-1pm

Have special circumstances? Let us know, our team will do our best to accommodate you.

Compression Stockings & Socks

We provide prescription compression stockings and socks for patients who need support for circulation or swelling. Please visit us in person for professional measurements prior to ordering.
Call ahead to schedule a fitting at your convenience.

Vaccinations and Immunizations

Our certified pharmacists are trained and experienced in administering a variety of vaccinations and immunizations to help protect you and your family from preventable diseases.
Please note: some specialized immunizations may require a referral or a visit to a healthcare provider. Feel free to ask our staff about which vaccines are available and how we can assist you in staying up to date.
